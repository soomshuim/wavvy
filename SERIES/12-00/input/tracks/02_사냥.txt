Track 02: 사냥 (Hunt)
Type: A
BPM: 105
Key: F Major
Length: 3min+

=== STYLE ===
Korean Afro Neo-Soul, 105 BPM, F Major,
Rhodes electric piano lead, talking drum accents,
subtle log drum texture, FMaj7-Gm7-Am7 progression,
African groove percussion, syncopated Afro rhythm,
warm bass groove, light guitar stabs,
Male vocal: raw, solid, direct, soulful, chest voice,
groovy, uplifting, midday energy,
articulation, moderate reverb, EQ balanced sound, clean mix

Lead vocal remains single and dominant throughout. No stacked or choir-like harmonies.
Chorus expands through rhythm and bass movement, not vocal layering.

Verse: Rhodes groove + talking drum, conversational
Verse2: energy build, bass movement
Chorus: rhythm lift, groovy peak
Outro: instrumental fade

=== EXCLUDE ===
EDM drops, heavy 808, synth bass,
falsetto, whisper, airy vocal, backing vocals,
full Amapiano, log drum dominant,
dark, melancholic, minor key feel,
harmonized, choir, vocoder
